About the city :

Lucknow, the capital of Uttar Pradesh extends along the banks of the river Gomti. The creator of Lucknow as it is today was Nawab Asaf ud Daula. The city became known as a centre for Urdu poetry and courty diction and reached its acount during the reign of Wajid Ali Shah who was a connoisseur of music and poetry.
